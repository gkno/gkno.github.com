  • About
    Who are we?
    We are a group of tool developers from the Marthlab at Boston College. We create software for the analysis of next-generation sequencing data.
    More Info
    What is this?
    gkno is an effort to create a set of NGS resources to guide users in navigating the sometimes tricky world of cutting edge NGS software. Here you'll find pipelines and tutorials that provide optimal solutions for the analysis of diverse problems in a wide variety of organisms.
