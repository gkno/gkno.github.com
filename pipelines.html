Pipelines
We took inspiration from our own subway system here in Boston ( the T ) to create pipeline maps. Just as the T map helps you get from point A to point B, the pipeline maps help you take what you have (e.g a fastq file) and get to where you want to go (e.g. a vcf) while clearly illustrating all the stops along the way. Though we only have the red (pipe)line running at the moment, more lines will come online in the future.
